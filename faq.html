FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your session.

How do I book a session? +
You can book a session by reaching out directly via the contact form or WhatsApp. Once your preferred date is confirmed, a deposit is required to secure your slot.
What should I wear for my headshot? +
Stick to clean, well-fitted outfits in solid colors. Avoid busy patterns or large logos. You can bring 2–3 options, and we'll choose what works best on camera.
Do you guide posing during the session? +
Yes. You don't need prior experience — I'll guide you through poses, angles, and expressions to ensure you look natural, confident, and professional.
What's the payment structure? +
A deposit is required to confirm your booking, with the balance paid before or on the day of the session.
Can I reschedule my session? +
Yes, rescheduling is allowed with prior notice. Please inform me as early as possible to secure a new date.
Do you travel for shoots? +
Yes, I'm available for on-location sessions. Please note that bookings outside the studio attract an additional logistics fee, which will be determined before booking.
What happens after the session? +
After your session, you'll receive a selection gallery to choose your preferred images. Your selected photos will then be professionally retouched and delivered within the agreed timeframe.
